Identifying Your Requirements

Before starting any project, you should do a lot of research to make sure it will be a success. We take into consideration your desires for your basement living space. Do you want to create a gathering place for friends and family, such as a bar or entertainment area? Are you interested in a wine cellar, home gym, or home theater? Or perhaps you need a pantry or guest room? If you're looking for an apartment that generates income, we can help you with that too. Contact us, and we will start turning your vision into reality.

Design Center Consultation

Virtual Design Consultation

Our experienced designers will look at your wish list and our extensive knowledge of basement design to come up with the most creative and useful 3D designs for your basement. You will have the opportunity to make adjustments in real-time, bringing your vision to life. They will give a full rundown of all the features and parts that come with our all-in-one package of high-quality building materials. Once the design is perfect, we will present the complete agreement and payment structure. We also provide affordable financing options.

Early-Stage Inspection

Walk-Through Technical Inspection (Site Visit)

Our designer and project manager will look at your space and design idea, as well as the layout of the plumbing, electrical, and mechanical systems. We will keep track of any changes or decisions made since the virtual design appointment that need to be made to the design. All of this information will be used to create the final design, which will serve as the construction project's manual.

Choose Finishes

Our designers will help you choose from a wide range of premium finishes and add-ons to make your basement look and feel like you.

The Build
